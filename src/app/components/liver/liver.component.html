LIVER TEST FORM

Liver disease encompasses various conditions that impact the normal functioning of the liver. Causes can include viral infections, excessive alcohol consumption, fatty liver disease, autoimmune disorders, genetic factors, or certain medications. Complications can range from liver cirrhosis to liver failure or cancer. Symptoms vary but may include fatigue, jaundice, abdominal pain, swelling, and changes in appetite or weight. Management involves early detection, lifestyle changes, medication, and sometimes liver transplantation. Prevention measures include regular check-ups, hepatitis vaccination, and avoiding toxins. Education about risk factors and liver health is vital in preventing and effectively managing liver disease.
