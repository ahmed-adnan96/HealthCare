Heart disease encompasses various conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels, and it is a leading cause of global mortality. Risk factors include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, obesity, poor diet, inactivity, diabetes, and family history. Symptoms can include chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, and palpitations. Management involves early detection, lifestyle changes, medication, and medical interventions when necessary. Education and prevention play significant roles in reducing the impact of heart disease and promoting heart health.
